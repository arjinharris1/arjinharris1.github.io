About me

Arjin Harris

Hello my name is Arjin. I am originally from the middle of nowhere Georgia. As you can see, I like to spend my free time competing in mud races, well pre covid era. I have completed two Spartan race Trifecta medals. Additionally, I am a co-owner of a small seafood restaurant called Cajun Boil Bar. I spent a little over 6 years in the army and I am an Ohio State alum. My degree is in theoretical Mathematics weird I know, but the biggest influences in my life have been math teachers so it just came naturally. I spend the first few years of my professional career running the operations side of a small Tech company here in Columbus called TicketFire. I picked up quite a few skills there
because it was a small company, and everyone had to where multiple hats. I also spent a good part of my time there acting as a go between for our software team and our customers. I really enjoyed turning customer input into actionable items for them to develop. It was very thrilling see projects come to fruition. I figured it was time for me to dive all the way in and here we are! Whether you are looking for a new junior dev or want talk races over some crab legs feel free to drop me a line.
